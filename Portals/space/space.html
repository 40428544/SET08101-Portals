PORTALS

You find yourself in a spaceship, floating in the middle of the void. The portal that you came from is still open, you also see another spaceship, further away, with the evil villain symbol stamped on it.

To your right a black hole is slowly sucking in all around and your spaceship with it. To the left a new portal has been opened. At the same time, an incoming communication is awaiting for your approval. Decide quick hero, destiny awaits.
