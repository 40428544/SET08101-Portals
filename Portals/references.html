PORTALS

The world needs a hero. A playful evil time-traveler has stolen a powerful weapon that has to be found to avoid destruction and save our planet.

Luckily we have a time machine to jump through the space-time continuum. However, there are tricks, traps and hidden clues along the way, you will have to use your astuteness and ingenuity to overcome many obstacles.

If you decide to embark this journey be sure to be prepared for challenging times. Humanity will be in debt with you forever traveler, and if you were to come back alive, great rewards will be waiting...
