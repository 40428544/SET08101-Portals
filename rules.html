PORTALS

These rules can be followed as original version of the game, for exploration is suggested to skip some of them, at your convenience.

  • Avoid browser forward and backward buttons.
  • Use buttons provided by the game and avoid duplicating tabs.
  • When possible, use pen and paper, there are stuff to remember.
  • If 'Restart Game' is pressed any progress made will be lost.

For further information about the game on how was made or source to ideas that helped develop it go to 'References'.
